Donations

DaySpring is a self-funded initiative of HighPoint Community Services Association (HCSA). HCSA is a member of the National Council of Social Services and a recognised Institute of Public Character (IPC).

DaySpring relies on the generous support of individuals and organizations whose donations help to fund the operating costs of the Residential Treatment Centre. A cash donation will go a long way in helping abused teenage girls make peace with their past, and find renewed hope and purpose in life.

Please provide us with your name, NRIC No/Company Reg No, address, contact number and email and the amount that you wish to donate if you are going for tax relief (Only applicable in Singapore).

You will be forwarded to Paypal to make the transaction securely.

Do take note that you would need to have a credit card or a paypal account to donate.

Thank you for your generous contribution.
